Detailed Description

Indices for the color channels in a single pixel.

E.g. for a 24-bit RGB bitmap (8 bits per channel), the individual components for the pixel at address unsigned char * p may be accessed as p[Channel::RED], p[Channel::GREEN] and p[Channel::BLUE].
