Detailed Description

Solve logistic regression problems.

The goal is to fit the logistic curve y = 1 / (1 + exp(-a - b.x)) to data points (x, y). We follow the linearization approach of:

J. Mathews, "Bounded population growth: a curve fitting lesson", Math. and Comput. Educ. J. 26(2), pp. 169-176, Spring 1992.
