Update a part of (a face of) the texture from a portion of a pixel buffer.

The block of the source image with corner (src_x, src_y, src_z) and size src_width x src_height x src_depth is copied to the corresponding block of the texture with corner (dst_x, dst_y, dst_z). The face argument is ignored for non-cube map textures.
