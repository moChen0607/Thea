BestFitEllipsoid3 ( Real  eps_ = 0.05)

Constructor.

The optional argument (which may be ignored by the implementation) controls the approximation ratio (1 + eps_) of the computed ellipsoid. A smaller value gives a better fit but may take longer to compute.

void getAxes ( Vector3 &  axis0,
Vector3 &  axis1,
Vector3 &  axis2 
) const

Get the axes of the ellipsoid.

The lengths of the axes represent the shape of the ellipsoid. The axes will always be returned in decreasing order of length, i.e. |axis0| >= |axis1| >= |axis2|. Also, (axis0.cross(axis1)).normalized() == axis2.normalized().
