void begin ( )

Start building the mesh.

For every begin there must be a corresponding end(). Depending on the type of mesh, you should not try to modify the mesh through other means until the corresponding call to end(). A mesh may be incrementally built in piecewise fashion through multiple begin() / end() blocks. Blocks may not be nested.
