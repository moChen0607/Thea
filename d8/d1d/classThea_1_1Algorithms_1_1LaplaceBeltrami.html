Detailed Description

Compute the discrete Laplace-Beltrami operator of a mesh.

This only works when the mesh vertices are standard 3-vectors (with dot and cross products defined). The vertices, corresponding to rows (and columns) of the matrix are numbered in sequential order (according to the vertex iterator of the mesh).

Two algorithms are provided for computing the L-B operator:

  • Guoliang Xu, "Discrete Laplace-Beltrami Operator on Sphere and Optimal Spherical Triangulations". Int. J. Comput. Geometry Appl. 16(1): 75-93 (2006).
  • M. Belkin, J. Sun, Y. Wang, "Discrete Laplace Operator for Meshed Surfaces", Proc. SoCG (2008).
