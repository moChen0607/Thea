Detailed Description

template<typename MeshT, typename Enable = void>
class Thea::Algorithms::MeshVertexTriple< MeshT, Enable >

A set of three vertices of a single face of a mesh.

Works for general and DCEL meshes.

See also
GeneralMesh, DCELMesh
